Hello! You made it to my website! My name is Peyton Ellis, but you can call me Sille <3
Let me tell you a little bit about myself... Growing up, I spent my free time listening to various CDs in our living room and danced til my eyes got heavy. I would pause the song and change my outfit to match the music and internalize the rhythm. My parents put me in dance classes at age 4 and music lessons at age 7. I've always loved skits and playing a role, but I hated acting classes. I was very moody, so when I began to write my own music, my cancer moon self was fulfilled.
